selling sustainable future with Coconut Waste

At EcoPuls, we are India’s first startup focused on creating gunny bags and packaging materials from coir fiber — a strong, sustainable, and biodegradable alternative to jute and plastic. Born out of the need to reduce agricultural waste and revive local coir industries, our mission is to transform coir into industrial-grade, eco-friendly products that support farmers, empower rural communities, and serve businesses across agriculture, logistics, and packaging. From waste to innovation, we are redefining how India sees coir — not just as waste, but as a resource for the future.

Nature. Purpose. Performance.

Made from natural coconut coir, our products are built for real-world use — tough, reusable, and biodegradable. Every item is crafted to protect the planet while serving India’s industrial needs.

Tradition Meets Innovation.

We combine the wisdom of India’s coir heritage with new-age product design and automated machinery to bring eco-smart packaging solutions that work — from farms to factories.
